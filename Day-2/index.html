😎 Welcome to CodeJourney: HTML Edition

So... you want to learn HTML? Great choice! 🎉 HTML is like the skeleton of every website. Without it, the internet would just be... blank. But don’t worry, this isn’t a boring textbook. In fact, HTML is going to talk to you today. Yep — the tags are alive. 😲 They’ll introduce themselves, explain what they do, and maybe crack a few jokes.

Ready to meet the crew? Let’s go!

Hey there! I’m <h1> — The Loudest Tag in Town!

When you want to shout something to the world (like “BREAKING NEWS”), you call me. I'm the big boss of titles.

Hi! I'm <h2> — a little quieter than <h1>

Think of me like the section heading in a book. Loud, but not screaming.

Yo, I'm <h3>

I work under h2. We’re a team. You can keep going down to h6... but I get no respect there. 😅

📝 I’m a <p> tag — I love talking!

Everything you're reading now is inside me. I’m the one who turns your thoughts into readable chunks. Like this one!

Want to break a line
like this? Just use the <br> tag. Easy!

🔗 I’m a Link (<a>) — Let’s Go Places!

Click me and I’ll teleport you to another page. I’m basically a hyperlink superhero.

🖼️ Meet <img> — I show off cool pics

I never talk — I just show. Add an alt tag in case I don’t load. Like my backup plan.
